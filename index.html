Welcome to the Arlington Heights Garden Club

The Arlington Heights Garden Club is a community organization welcoming all levels of gardeners. We provide a forum to educate, share knowledge and experience, and encourage the love of gardening while fostering friendships. Our club promotes, protects and encourages environmental and conservation best practices. We provide community guidance for civic beautification and support students and other non-profits.

Our Mission  

The Arlington Heights Garden Club is a community organization welcoming all levels of gardeners. We provide a forum to educate, share knowledge and experience, and encourage the love of gardening while fostering friendships. Our club promotes, protects and encourages environmental and conservation best practices. We provide community guidance for civic beautification and support students and other non-profits. 

The Arlington Heights Garden Club keeps the Village in full bloom 

  •   The Arlington Heights Garden Club holds monthly meetings with knowledgeable speakers offering informational and practical advice to both the beginner and experienced gardener.
  • We plan multiple events for the community including the Garden Walk and Garden Sales at the library.  
  • The Arlington Heights Garden Club is now accepting applications from graduating high school seniors or college students from the Northwest suburbs who are or plan to pursue studies in Horticulture, Environmental Science, Sustainability, Conservation, Arboriculture, and similar studies.

Being a member of this gardening club has brought me so much joy and satisfaction over the years.I have always loved tending to my garden, but it wasn't until I joined this club that I truly began to appreciate the beauty and intricacy of gardening. Jim Bob

I am writing to express my deep gratitude for the gardening club that changed my life. When I first joined, I was merely looking for a new hobby to occupy my time. What I found was so much more than that.Through this club, I discovered a passion for gardening that I never knew existed within me. The knowledge and skills I have gained from my fellow members have not only transformed my garden but also my life. I find myself spending more time outdoors, more attuned to the rhythms of nature, and more fulfilled than I ever thought possible.

Marty

Joining this club has been one of the best decisions I've made in recent years.
